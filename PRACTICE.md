# Piano Keys Practice

Open this file beside the app and play the letters exactly as written.

## How To Read This

```text
a b c      play one after another
a--        press a, then wait a little
a---       press a, then wait longer
a+b+c      press together as a chord
REST       wait, press nothing
FAST(...)  play the notes inside quicker
```

Do not hold every key. Tap normally unless you see `--` or `---`.

## Harry Potter Theme

Practice slowly. The real tune feeling comes from the pauses.

### Part 1

```text
f-- l x z
l--- n-- v z
l-- x z k
;--- f---
```

### Part 2

```text

l--- n-- . ,

```

### Part 1 + Part 2 Together

```text

f-- l x z  l--- n-- . ,

```

## Optional Bass Hits

Add one bass hit before a melody line if you want a fuller sound.

```text
1+5+8     f-- l x z  l--- n-- v z
e+u+p     l-- x z k  ;--- f---
g+l+x     f-- l x z  l--- n-- . ,
f+l+n     g-- c m n  b-- z x l---
```

## Practice Order

1. Learn `Part 1`.
2. Learn `Part 2`.
3. Play `Part 1 + Part 2 Together`.
4. Add the `Faster Ending Practice`.
5. Try the `Optional Bass Hits`.
